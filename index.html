Day 1: Fazail-e-Ramzan

Welcome to Day 1 content.

Nahmaduhuu nusalli alaa rasuulihil kareemi haamidanw wamusalliyanw wa musallimaa.

نَحْمَدُهُ وَنُصَلّى عَلَى رَسُولِهِ الْكَرِيمِ حَامِدًا وَمُصَلِّيَا وَ مُسَلِّمًا.

Hamd-o-Salaat ke baad ye channd ahadees ka tarjuma hai jo Ramzan-ul-Mubarak ke baare mein waarid hui hain. Nabi-e-Kareem ki Rahmat-ul-lil-Aalameen zaat ne musalmaanon ke liye har baab mein jis qadar fazail aur targhibaat irshaad farmayi hain, inka asal shukriya aur qadar-daani to ye thi ke hum in par mar mitten, magar hamari kotahiyaon aur deeni be-raghbatiyan is qadar roz-afzon hain ke, un par amal to darkinaar inki taraf iltifaat aur tawajoh bhi nahi rahi. Hattake ab logaon ko in ka ilm bhi bahut kam hogaya hai.


In auraaq ka maqsad ye hai ke, agar masajid ke a'immah, tarawih ke huffaaz aur wo padhe likhe hazraat jinko deen ki kisi darja mein bhi raghbat hai, awail-e-Ramzan mein is risaale ko masajid aur majmu'e mein suna diya kare, to Allah ki rahmat se kya ba'eed hai ke apne mahboob ke kalaam ki barkat se hum logaon ko mubarak mahine ki kuch qadar aur iski barkaat ki taraf kuch tawajoh hojaaya kare aur neik amal ki ziyadati, aur bad-amaliyon ki kami ka zariya ban jaaya kare. Huzoor ka irshaad hai ke, agar Haq Ta'ala Shanahu, teri wajah se ek shakhs ko bhi hidayat farmadein to tere liye surkh oontniyaon se (jo umdah maal shumaar hota hai) behtar aur afzal hai.


Ramzan-ul-Mubarak ka mahina musalmaanon ke liye Haq Ta'ala Shanahu, ka bahut hi bada inaam hai, magar jab hi, ke is inaam ki qadar bhi ki jaaye. Warna hum se mehromaon ke liye ek mahina tak Ramzan, Ramzan chillaye jaane ke siwa kuch bhi nahi. Ek hadees mein hai ke, agar logaon ko ye maloom hojaaye ke Ramzan kya cheez hai to meri ummat ye tamanna kare ke saara saal Ramzan hi hojaaye.

Ek hadees mein irshaad hai ke, Ramzan-ul-Mubarak ke roze aur har mahine mein teen roze rakhna dil ki buhat si halaton aur ghamon ko door karta hai. Akhir ko to baat hai ke Sahaba-e-Karam رضی اللہ عنہم ke maqam aur rutbe samajh kar unhein roze rakhne ke maalek bana diya gaya. Hazrat-e-Huzoor ﷺ ne bhee apne chehnaam karaye. Hazrat-e-Huzoor ﷺ ka irshaad hai, log is tamanna mein lagein.


Muslim Shareef ki ek hadees mein hai ke, Sahaba رضی اللہ عنہم ek arwaak karte hain, ke saal par utres, gunah bakhsh diye jayein, jinka haath ro mujhe aise laga ke makaan se kharij ho jaye. Ek hadees mein hai ke Ramzan-ul-Mubarak ke fazail aur uske ehkaam ke muqable mein Huzoor ﷺ ka irshaad hai ke log is tamanna mein roze rakh kar dil ki bohot si halaton se bakhabar ho jaayein.

Nabi-e-Kareem ﷺ se sekdon wazaayein mein mukhtalif anwaak ke izhaal ki gayi, jinka haath ro mujhe aise laga ke makaan se kharij ho jaye.

Hazrat-e-Shanaahu ne irshaad farmaya, jo shakhs apni halat mein taghayur aur inqilab chahta hai, to meri yeh baat samajh lo ke is shakhs par roze rakhna aur har mahine mein roze rakhna dil ko rehta hai. Isliye 21 hadees par iktifa karte hain, aur inko teen faslon par munqasim rakhte hain.


Pehle fasl mein Ramzan-ul-Mubarak ke fazail mein dus, teesre fasl mein saath ahadees hain. Teesri fasl mein itikaaf ka zikr hai, is fasl mein teen ahadees hain. Is ke baad khaatim mein ek taweel hadees par is risale ko khaatam kar diya. Haq Ta'ala Shanaahu hum sab ko iski barakat se qubool, ramzanwein aur mujhi sparaanwein ko iski barkat se intifaak ki toufeeq ataa farmaawen.

Day 2: Fazail-e-Ramzan

Welcome to Day 2 content.

FASL-E-AWWAL
Fazail-e-Ramzan mein
Hazrat Salman رضی اللہ عنہ kehte hain ke, Nabi-e-Kareem ﷺ ne Shabaan ki aakhri tareekh mein hum logon ko farmaya, "Ke tumhare oopar ek mahaina aaraha hai, jo bohat bada mahaina hai, bohat mubarak mahaina hai. Is mein ek raat hai (Shab-e-Qadr) jo hazaaron mahinon se behtar hai. Allah Ta'ala ne is ke roza ko farz farmaya aur uske raat ke qiyaam (yaani Taraweeh) ko sawaab ki cheez banaya hai. Jo shakhs is mahine mein kisi neiki ke saath Allah ka qurb hasil kare aisa hai jaise ke ghair Ramzan mein farz ada kiya aur jo shakhs is mahine mein kisi farz ko ada kare, wo aisa hai jaise ke ghair Ramzan mein 70 farz ada kare. Ye mahina sabar ka hai aur sabar ka badla jannat hai aur ye mahina logon ke saath ghamkhwari karne ka hai. Is mahine mein momin ka rizq badha diya jata hai. Jo shakhs kisi rozedaar ka roza iftar karaye iske liye gunaahon ke mu'af hone aur aag se khalasi ka sabab hoga aur rozedaar ke sawaab ki manind isko sawaab hoga. Magar us rozedaar ke sawaab se kuchh kam nahi kiya jaayega."Sahaba رضی اللہ عنہم ne arz kiya ke, "Ya Rasool Allah ﷺ! Hum mein se har shakhs to itni was'at nahi rakhta ke rozedaar ko iftar karaye, to Aap ﷺ ne farmaya ke (pait bhar khilane par mauqoof nahi) ye sawab to Allah ek khajoor se koi iftar karaade ya ek ghoont paani pilawae ya ek ghoont lassi pilawae us par bhi marhamat farma dete hain. Ye aisa mahina hai ke iska awwal hissa rehmat, darmiyani hissa maghfirat aur aakhri hissa aag se azaadi hai. Haq Ta'ala Shanahu apne ghulaam wo khaadim ko bakhsh dete hain aur iski maghfirat farmate hain. Aur doosri cheezon ki is mein kasrat hai aur azaadi mein se do cheezen Allah Ta'ala ki rizq ke waaste aur do cheezen aisi hain ke, jin se tumhe chara kaar nahi. Pehli do cheezenin jin tum apne Rabb ko raazi karo wo kalima-e-tayyiba aur istighfar ki kasrat hai. Aur doosri do cheezenin ye hai ke, jin ki talab karo aur aag se panaah maango jo shakhs kisi rozedaar ko paani pilaye, Haq Ta'ala Shanahu (qayamat ke din) mere hoze se isko aisa paani pilayenge jis ke baad jannat mein daakhil hone tak pyaas nahi lagegi.


Muhaddiseen ko iske baaz riwaayat mein kalaam hai. Lekin awwal to fazail mein is qadar kalaam qaabil-e-tahamul hai, doosre is ke aksar mazameen ki doosri riwaayat mu'ayyad hain. Is hadees se chand umoor maloom hote hain Pehla Nabi-e-Kareem ﷺ ka ehteemaam ke Shabaan ki aakhri tareeq mein khas taur se iska waaz farmaya aur logon ko tambih farmaayi, ta'ake Ramzan-ul-Mubarak ke second bhi ghaflat se na guzar jaaye. Phir waaz mein tamaam mahine ki fazilat bayaan farmaane ke baad chand aham umoor ki taraf khaas taur se mutwajeh farmaya. Sab se awwal shab-e-qadr ke, wo haqeeqat mein baith hi hai. Is raat hai in aayaq mein iska bayaan, doosri fasl mein mustaqil aayega. Iske baad irshad hai ke, Allah ne inke roze ko farz kiya aur iske qiyam yani taraweeh ko sunnat kiya. Is se maloom hua ke taraweeh ka irshad bhi khud Sub'haana wa Taqadus ki taraf se hai. Phir jin riwaayat mein Nabi-e-Kareem ﷺ isko apni taraf mansoob farmaya ke, mai ne sunnat kiya, un se muraad takeed hai. Huzoor ﷺ iski takeed bahut farmaate the. Isi wajah se sab ummat uske sunnat hone par muttafiq hain. Burhan mein likha hai ke musalmaanon mein se rawaafiz ke siwa, koi shakhs iska munkir nahi.


Hazrat Maulana Shah Abdul Haq Sahab Muhaddis Dehelvi Rahmatullah Alaih ne Masabata bis-Sunnah mein baaz kutub-e-fiqh se naqal kiya hai ke, kisi shehar ke log agar taraweeh chhod de to, iske chhodne se musalmaanon mein muqabla kare. Is jagah khusoosiyat se ek baat ka lehaaz rakhne ki zaroorat hai, wo ye ke bahut se logon ka khayal hota hai, ke kisi masjid mein aat, dus din mein kalaam-e-majeed sunlein phir chutti. Ye khayal rakhne ki baat hai ke, kalaamullah shareef ka taraweeh mein padna ya sunna ye mustaqil sunnat hai aur poore Ramzan shareef ki taraweeh mein mustaqil sunnat hai. Pas is surat mein ek sunnat par amal hua aur doosri reh gayi. Albata jin logon ko Ramzan-ul-Mubarak mein safar waghera ya kisi wajah se ek jagah rozana taraweeh padhna mushkil ho, inke liye munasib hai ke awwal Qur'an Shareef chand roz mein sunlein ta'ake Qur'an Shareef naqis na rahe, phir jahan waqt mila aur mauqa wahan taraweeh padhlik, ke Qur'an Shareef bhi is surat mein naqis nahi hoga aur apne kaam ka bhi harj na hoga.


Ibaadaat-e-Nafilah:

Huzoor ﷺ ne roze aur taraweeh ka hukum farmane ke baad aam farz aur nafil ibaadat ke ehtemaam ki taraf mutwajeh farmaya, yaani ke nafil ka sawab doosre mahino ke farz ke barabar hai aur iske ek farz ka sawab doosre mahino ke sattar (70) farz ke barabar hai. Is jagah hum logon ko apni apni ibaadat ke taraf zara ghaur karne ki zaroorat hai ke, is Mubarak mahine mein farz ka hum se kis qadar ehtemaam hota hai aur nawaafil mein kitna izaafa hota hai. Farz mein to hamare ehtemaam ki ye haalat hai ke seher khaanay ke baad jo sote hain to aksar subah ki namaz qaza hogayi. Aur kam az kam jama'at to aksaron ki faut hi hojati hai, goya seher khaanay ka shukr adaa kiya ke Allah ke sab farz chhod diye. Se zyada mohtam bish-shan farz ko ya bilkul qaza kardiya ya kam az kam naqis kardiya ke baghair jama’at ke namaz padhne ko ahl-e-usool ne adaa-e-naaqis farmaya hai. Aur Huzoor Akram ﷺ ka to ek jagah irshaad hai ke masjid ke qareeb rehne walon ko goya namaz baghair masjid ke hoti hi nahi. Mazahir-e-haq, mein likha hai ke jo shakhs baghair jama’at bidun-e-jama’at namaz padhta hai, iske zimme se farz to saqit hojata hai magar isko namaz ka sawab nahi milta. Isi tarah agar doosri namaz maghrib ki bhi jama’at aksaron ki iftaar ki naza ke badle mein aur raka’at-e-oola ya takbeer-e-oola ka to zikr hi hai. Aur bahut se log to isha ki namaz hi taraweeh ke ehsan ke badle mein waqt se pehle hi padhllete hain. Ye to Ramzan-ul-Mubarak mein hamari namaz ka haal hai jo aam tareen farizon mein hai ke ek farz ke badle mein teen ko zaaya kiya, aksar hi aksar hi warna zohar ki namaz qailoolah ki nazar aur asar ki jama’at iftaari ka samaan khareedne ki nazaar hote hue namaaz dekha gaya hai. Isi tarah aur faraiz par aap khud ghaur farmaalein ke kitna ehtemaam Ramzan-ul-Mubarak mein inka kiya jaata hai aur jab faraiz ka ye haal hai to nawafil ka kya poochna. Ishraq aur chaasht to Ramzan-ul-Mubarak mein sone ki nazaar ho jaate hain aur Awaabeen ka kaise ehtemaam hosakta hai, jab ke abhi roza khola hai aur ainda taraweeh ka seham hai, aur tahajjud ka waqt to hai hi ain seher khaane ka waqt phir nawafil ki gunjaish hi kahan? Lekin ye sab baatein be-tawajahi aur na karne ki hain ke tu hi agar na chaahte to baatein hazaar hain.
